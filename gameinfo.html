How to Play

Learn the rules and discover all the features of Telephone Game Challenge

1

Enter Your Name

Start by telling us who you are. The game remembers your profile, game history, and achievements across sessions using localStorage.

2

Remember the Message

You'll see a message appear on screen. The message will also be read aloud automatically. Listen carefully and remember every word!

3

Type or Speak

Choose your preferred input method. Type the message exactly as you remember it, or use the microphone to speak it naturally.

4

Pass It On

The next player receives your version of the message. Watch how the message transforms as it passes through the chain of players!

Game Features

Voice Recognition
Speak your message naturally using the Web Speech API. Our speech recognition works in real-time and supports multiple accents. Perfect for practising pronunciation!
Text-to-Speech
Messages are automatically read aloud in natural-sounding voices. This simulates the real telephone game experience where you only hear the message once.
Multiple Voices
Choose from 5 unique voice types: Male, Female, Girl, Old Woman, and Old Man. Each voice has different pitch and character, adding variety to each game.
English Levels
Four difficulty levels: B1 (80% speed), B2 (100% speed), C1 (120% speed), C2 (140% speed). Adjust speech speed to match your listening comprehension level.
Achievement Certificates
Earn 7 unique certificates for reaching milestones: First Steps, Sharp Memory (80%), Perfect Recall (90%), Photographic Memory (100%), Enthusiast (5 games), Veteran (10 games), Legend (25 games).
Game History
All your games are saved automatically. Track original messages, final messages, accuracy scores, dates, and categories. See your improvement over time!
Word Categories
Choose from 5 categories: Easy (25 words), Medium (15 sentences), Hard (15 complex sentences), Funny (15 jokes), Movies (15 famous quotes). Never play the same game twice!
Dark Mode
Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable gameplay day or night. Your preference is saved in your browser automatically.

Pro Tips

  • Use headphones for better audio clarity when messages are read aloud
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace for best voice recognition results
  • Start with Easy category and B1 level if you're a beginner
  • Challenge yourself with Hard category and C2 speed as you improve
  • Check your certificates to track your progress and achievements
  • Use keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+Enter to submit, Space to speak, N for next round
